EMPOWERING AND INSPIRING THE YOUTH


Created by Grade 10 students from Holy Family School Q.C who aspire to be future servant leaders in the community, Kaleidoscope is a Blog wherein the youth can join and partake in. In our community, we aim to cater to the needs of the youth, particularly the struggling students and teenagers looking for a meaningful accompaniment for their future that awaits them.

The Kaleidoscope team designed a safe and open space to empower and inspire, which is one of the greatest achievements our team could make because, in such a way, we are able to cultivate the minds and spirits of the next generation, the beacon of hope for the future.
