Balancing Rest and Studies

Author: Jamie Porca

Remember to never feel guilty in taking a rest just because of the overwhelming deadlines to chase. You might think in the future that you will regret resting but you will regret the negative effects of not resting more.

A student's biggest enemy is time. Hours spent on studies, tasks, and personal life is hard to balance. Time on leisure shouldn't weigh more than time for school, and vice versa.

Students often feel overwhelmed with the stack of tasks to be completed within a deadline. That is why the balance for personal life and studies is disrupted. It is important to take a productive approach in order to complete it one step at a time.  This could also serve as a lesson to improve one's discipline in work and studies. But how does one manage to do so?

  1. 1.Set realistic and achievable goals
  2. 2. Plan it to stay on track
  3. 3. Remember that such change will not be consistent at first. You must practice it until it is part of your routine.
  4. 4. If your plans are not effective, then adjust it.
