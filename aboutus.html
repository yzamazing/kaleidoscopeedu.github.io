Created by students from Grade 10 St. Clare Of Assisi, we uphold the famillian Core Values such as self respect,
compassion, honesty, excellence, leadership through service, and care for creation in our mission and vision. We are
powered by the youth, while empowering the youth.


Kaleidoscope is a website developed to be able to cover interests of people including such topics like mental health,
time management, national events, overseas updates, and more which are timely and relevant
mostly to students who are our target audience. This interactive website provides entertaining features
like daily polls and articles that updates users with the hot topics within the time.


Our main target upon creating this website is to create a better social, to discover one’s talent by helping them
find more of their interests. By using this website we affirm to provide beneficial information,
utilizing reliable resources and assistance. We made use of diversity, as what the word defines,
we have variety of contents covered within the page, made to fulfill the website's duties for the users.
